# Monetization Strategy — Language-learning-app

## Goal
語学学習アプリで収益化を実現する。

## Core insight
Mainstream English vocabulary and general JLPT markets are large but crowded. A better wedge is a narrow market where learners pay because language ability is tied to a concrete outcome: visa, employment, exam, placement, or workplace safety.

## Selected first target
**JFT-Basic + Specified Skilled Worker practical Japanese**

### Target users
- People outside Japan preparing to work in Japan
- Students in Japanese-language schools who need practical employment Japanese
- Workers preparing for Specified Skilled Worker routes
- Sending organizations and support organizations
- Care facilities / employers needing training support

## Product structure

### Free starter
- 30–80 cards
- JFT-Basic orientation
- Daily life phrases
- Basic workplace instructions
- Simple listening cards

### Paid pack 1
**JFT/SSW Core 500–800**
- Core vocabulary
- Situation phrases
- Listening comprehension
- Instructions and warnings
- Mini mock-test style drills

### Paid pack 2
**Nursing Care Japanese 300–500**
- Caregiving actions
- Patient/resident communication
- Safety and emergency phrases
- Reporting and handover
- Polite workplace expressions

### Paid pack 3
**Manufacturing/Safety Japanese 300–500**
- Factory instructions
- Safety signs
- Hazard warnings
- Tools and workflow
- Reporting problems

## Pricing hypotheses
- Free starter: free
- Individual paid pack: low one-time price or subscription
- Bundle: JFT + Nursing Care / JFT + Manufacturing
- B2B license: per school/agency/facility

## Promotion

### B2C channels
- SEO landing pages in English and target languages
- YouTube Shorts / TikTok / Facebook Reels
- Facebook groups for working in Japan
- Telegram/WhatsApp communities
- App Store / Google Play ASO keywords

### B2B/B2B2C channels
- Japanese-language schools
- Sending organizations
- Registered support organizations
- Care facilities
- Small manufacturing employers

## SEO landing page examples
- JFT-Basic Japanese vocabulary
- Japanese for Specified Skilled Worker
- Nursing Care Japanese for Tokutei Ginou
- Work in Japan Japanese phrases
- Factory Japanese safety words

## Differentiation
- Purpose-specific, not general language study
- Practical workplace Japanese
- Multi-language explanations
- Cards + listening + situations
- Progress backup and export
- Optional Anki export

## Risks
- Exam structures can change.
- Official names and eligibility can change.
- App-store discovery may still be weak.
- Content quality and audio quality matter.

## Risk controls
- Weekly market monitoring.
- Keep content modular.
- Use official public sources for structure, not copied content.
- Validate with small landing pages before building huge datasets.
