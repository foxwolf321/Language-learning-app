About this app

This app helps Indonesian learners build practical Japanese for JFT-Basic, SSW, and everyday work-life situations in Japan.

Cards focus on useful meanings, readings, short phrases, signs, listening-ready sentences, and small information-search tasks. Japanese examples now show furigana-style support for common kanji so beginners are not blocked by words like 説明.

All learner-facing examples are original. Public exam and Can-do information is used only to guide level, domain, and task design; official questions and textbook text are not copied.
